Hi! I am a Doctoral Academy Fellow at the University of Arkansas - Fayetteville. I received my B.S. degrees in Applied Mathematics, Computer Science, and Computer Engineering at University of Arkansas, where my honors thesis was on improving Bayesian inference in graph convolutional networks for robust semi-supervised node classification. I am currently a second year PhD student in the [Social Awareness and Intelligent Learning (SAIL)](https://sail.uark.edu/) Lab advised by [Dr. Xintao Wu](http://www.csce.uark.edu/~xintaowu/) and my research interests lie in the areas of deep learning, causal inference, and generative modeling with applications to explainable and transparent AI. My past research experience includes semi-supervised image classification, nonnegative matrix factorizations for clustering, Bayesian graph representation learning, and natural language processing. Additionally, I was a lead research mentor for the [Army Educational Outreach Program (AEOP)](https://www.usaeop.com/) UNITE program where I taught research methodologies in data science and machine learning to high school students from underrepresented communities.
