I am currently a third year Ph.D. student in the [Social Awareness and Intelligent Learning (SAIL)](https://sail.uark.edu/) Lab advised by [Dr. Xintao Wu](http://www.csce.uark.edu/~xintaowu/). My research interests lie generally in the areas of causal inference, representation learning, and probabilistic generative modeling with applications in explainable and trustworthy AI. 

Previously, I recieved by B.S. in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics from the University of Arkansas. My past research experience includes nonnegative matrix factorizations for clustering, Bayesian graph machine learning, and natural language processing. I was a lead research mentor for the [Army Educational Outreach Program (AEOP)](https://www.usaeop.com/) UNITE program where I taught research methodologies in data science and machine learning to high school students from underrepresented communities.
